What GPS2SMS does:

1) Access to your contacts list to let you pick a contact and its phone number

2) Acquire device location using accurate method (GPS)

3) Send an SMS containing this location and the current time to the contact selected at step 1)

To achieve this, GPS2SMS needs permissions to access to contacts, to device location and to « send SMS » functionnality



What GPS2SMS does NOT:

  • establish a data connection

  • store information or share it to any 3rd party

  • show ads or ask for paid content



Disclaimer:

Do not use GPS2SMS for life-saving purposes.

In some places, GPS reception is poor and GPS2SMS may NOT be able to acquire phone's location.

In some places, network coverage is poor and GPS2SMS may NOT be able to send SMS.

When phone's battery power is low (like when depleted or at low temperature), GPS2SMS may NOT be able to acquire location and send SMS.
